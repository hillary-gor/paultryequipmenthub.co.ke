What types of poultry equipment do you supply?

We offer a wide range of poultry equipment including feeders, drinkers, incubators, brooders, cages, and ventilation systems. Our products cater to both small-scale and large-scale poultry farms.

Do you provide installation services for the equipment?

Yes, we provide professional installation services for all the equipment we supply. Our team ensures that the equipment is set up correctly and efficiently to meet your farm's needs.

Can I get a custom quote for my specific requirements?

Absolutely! You can contact us with your specific needs, and we will provide a customized quote tailored to your farm's requirements.

What is the warranty period for your equipment?

Our equipment comes with a standard warranty period of 12 months. Some products may have extended warranties, which will be specified at the time of purchase.

Do you offer maintenance and repair services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance and repair services to ensure your equipment remains in optimal condition. Regular maintenance can help extend the lifespan of your equipment and prevent unexpected breakdowns.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order through our website, by phone, or by visiting our store. Our customer service team is available to assist you with any questions and guide you through the ordering process.
